The Problem

Sourcing flowers is manual, time-consuming, and costly.

  • Two separate portals — logging in, comparing prices and availability by hand
  • Holiday guesswork — Valentine's, Mother's Day volume based on gut feeling and last year's memory
  • Over-ordering = waste — flowers don't wait. Perishable inventory is unforgiving.
  • Under-ordering = scrambling — emergency orders at premium prices, disappointed customers
  • Wedding planning friction — manually calculating stems per arrangement × number of tables × buffer
  • Hours lost weekly — time spent on procurement that could be spent designing and selling

Phase 1 · 2-3 Weeks

Smart Price & Availability Dashboard

🔗 Wholesaler Integration

Connects to Washington Floral (via Komet K2K API) and iBuyFlowers (web automation). Pulls real-time data on a schedule.

📊 Side-by-Side View

"Red roses — Washington: $0.85/stem, 500 avail. iBuy: $0.72/stem, 800 avail, ships Thursday."

🔔 Smart Alerts

Get notified when prices drop on your most-ordered stems, or when popular varieties are running low.

⚡ One-Click Order

Jump directly to the order page on whichever wholesaler has the best deal. No more portal-hopping.

Technical note: Washington Floral uses Komet Sales K2K — Komet has an API. iBuyFlowers requires browser automation or data feed partnership.

Phase 2 · 3-4 Weeks

AI Demand Forecasting Engine

  • Ingests past order history — even spreadsheets, receipts, or POS exports
  • Layers holiday calendars — Valentine's, Mother's Day, Easter, prom season, Thanksgiving, Christmas
  • Pulls in wedding bookings — 3 weddings June 14-15? Auto-calculates stem needs per package
  • Factors in weather data — heat shortens vase life, rain affects local supply
  • Learns your patterns — the more you use it, the smarter it gets

EXAMPLE AI RECOMMENDATION

"You have 2 weddings June 14-15 + normal Saturday traffic. Recommend ordering 400 roses, 200 peonies, 150 eucalyptus by June 9. Best price: iBuy for roses ($0.68/stem), Washington for peonies ($1.20/stem, local = fresher)."

Phase 3 · 1-2 Weeks

Automated Order Suggestions

  • Weekly "suggested order" notification — email or SMS, your choice
  • Factors in upcoming events, seasonal trends, current inventory, weather
  • One-click approve — or adjust quantities, then auto-submits to the right wholesaler
  • Splits orders intelligently — roses from iBuy (cheaper), greens from Washington (fresher, local)
  • Holiday mode: ramps up automatically 2-3 weeks before peak dates

📱 Monday morning notification:

"This week's order is ready. 3 wedding orders + Valentine's ramp-up. Total: $2,340 across 2 suppliers. ✅ Approve · ✏️ Edit"
